About Us

IIChE BITM

Established in 2013, the IIChE Student Chapter at BIT Mesra is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and professional excellence in the field of chemical engineering. The chapter serves as a dynamic platform where students connect academic knowledge with real-world industrial practices through workshops, expert lectures, industrial visits, technical sessions, and collaborative learning experiences. It also proudly organizes Coalescence, the chapter's flagship annual event, bringing together students, academicians, and industry professionals to celebrate learning, creativity, and engineering excellence. By promoting technical competence, teamwork, innovation, and professional growth, IIChE BIT Mesra empowers aspiring chemical engineers to develop industry-ready skills, embrace emerging technologies, and contribute meaningfully to the advancement of engineering and society.

Our Mission

To nurture technical excellence and professional leadership in chemical engineering. We conduct hands-on projects, industry visits, and research publications to prepare members for real-world impact.

Our Vision

To drive the transition towards sustainable process engineering. We aim to inspire sustainable chemical solutions that balance environmental preservation with industrial progress.

Our Culture

A collaborative environment where curiosity meets structure. We work across multi-disciplinary teams of designers, writers, and technical leads to build a highly active student community.
