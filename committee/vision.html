HOD, Chemical Engineering

Dr. G.T. Mohanraj

Dr. G.T. Mohanraj serves as the Head of the Department of Chemical Engineering at BIT Mesra. With over 15 years of excellent achievements in educational governance and chemical science research, he guides both the faculty and the undergraduate research board with distinction.

His educational philosophy encourages chemical process systems that prioritize sustainability and resource optimization. He plays an active role in coordinating institutional grants and scaling department testing labs to align with standard international benchmarks.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Polymer Nanocomposites & Functional Blends
  • Organic Bistable Device & Organic Electronics
  • Development of Smart Materials like Sensors & Actuators
  • Biomass-Derived Activated Carbon & Sustainable Materials

Author of 30+ peer-reviewed papers in high-impact international journals. Actively supervises national-level doctoral candidates and research scholars.

Academic Qualifications:

  • Ph.D. in Polymer Technology from IIT Kharagpur
  • M.Tech in Polymer Science & Technology from IIT Delhi
  • B.Tech in Chemical Engineering from University of Madras
  • Post-Doctoral Fellowships (PDF) in Italy, South Korea and France

Memberships & Panels:

Senior Advisor to academic curriculum boards, and active consultant for college based polymer processing labs.

Faculty Advisor, IICHE

Dr. Abhijit Mondal

Dr. Abhijit Mondal is a professor in Chemical Engineering and acts as the official Faculty Advisor and Coordinator of the IIChE BITM Student Chapter. He plays an essential role in coordinating student conferences, industrial webinars, and engineering symposia.

He is highly committed to student mentoring, assisting our project teams to apply thermodynamic principles and process optimizations to solve modern ecological and resource challenges.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Environmental & Bio-Process Engineering
  • Process Intensification & Mass Transfer
  • Environmental Polymer Degradation & Green Composites
  • Bioremediation & Wastewater Engineering

Recognized globally with multiple research publications and patents. He spearheads several government-sponsored and industrial research projects.

Academic Credentials:

  • Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata
  • M.Tech in Chemical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata
  • B.Tech in Chemical Engineering from WBUtech, Kolkata

Honors & Accolades:

Award-winning editor(NIT Agartala) and published researcher with a strong track record in chemical engineering. Successfully led key R&D projects for national bodies like DRDO and DST alongside prominent industrial partners.

The Board & Managerial Team

The IIChE BIT Mesra Board and Managerial Team is a passionate group of student leaders committed to driving the chapter's vision and ensuring excellence across every domain. Working closely with the core team, they plan and execute flagship events, workshops, industrial interactions, and chapter initiatives while fostering innovation, teamwork, and leadership. Beyond organizational responsibilities, the board actively nurtures members' technical, creative, communication, and management skills through diverse learning opportunities. Every tenure lasts one year, with leadership passed on through a rigorous process of dedication, performance, and merit, ensuring a legacy of excellence and continuous growth.

The Core Committee

The core committee is the execution backbone of IIChE BITM, translating our strategic vision into reality through dedicated operations, event coordination, and outreach. Comprising specialized groups in research, content, PR, and design, this team tackles real-world challenges that shape them into committed, adaptable chemical engineers.

In this dynamic environment, members refine their skills, embrace teamwork, and unlock their full potential. With a positive attitude and an open mind, they learn to innovate and excel.
