The Legacy of Coalescence

Where Innovation Meets Chemical Engineering Excellence

Coalescence'25 is the flagship annual event of the IIChE Student Chapter, BIT Mesra, bringing together innovation, collaboration, and engineering excellence under one roof. It serves as a dynamic platform where students, academicians, researchers, and industry professionals come together to exchange ideas, showcase talent, and explore the latest advancements in chemical engineering and allied fields. Through engaging competitions, expert talks, workshops, and interactive sessions, fosters creativity, inspires innovation, and empowers the next generation of engineers to solve real-world challenges while shaping a sustainable future.

Over three dynamic days, Coalescence'25 unites students, academicians, and industry professionals through competitions, workshops, expert talks, and interactive sessions. It is a platform to innovate, collaborate, showcase talent, and gain valuable insights into the ever-evolving world of chemical engineering.
